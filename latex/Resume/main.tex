\section*{Summary}
I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Purdue University, specializing in compilers. I have significant experience working on the LLVM compiler. I am experienced with MLIR, and compilers for GPU. My research interests lie in compiler optimizations, automatic parallelization, data layout optimization, compilers for GPU, security and software verification.
